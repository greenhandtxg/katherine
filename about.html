About Me

Katherine

My name is Katherine, and I am on a dedicated path from understanding mass communication to understanding the individual mind, with a clear goal of applying that knowledge to help others.

This space reflects my journey and professional growth, and this website serves as a professional portfolio and a space for reflection as I progress on my journey. It is a testament to my belief that effective communication is the first step toward healing and psychological well-being.

Education: Communication

I am currently completing my Bachelor of Communications at Curtin Singapore, specializing in Web Media and Marketing Communication. This program has provided me with a robust framework for understanding the systems people use to connect in our digital age. It has honed my skills in clear messaging, audience analysis, and the ethical use of media—all of which are foundational to effective and empathetic interaction.

Future Goals: Psychology and Counseling

My academic journey will not end with my bachelor’s degree. My next step is to pursue a Master’s in Psychology at the National University of Singapore. This advanced degree will equip me with the clinical knowledge and theoretical depth required to achieve my ultimate career goal: to work as a Psychological Counselor at the Fei Yue Family Service Centre here in Singapore. My specific focus will be on providing support to adolescents, helping them build resilience and navigate the unique difficulties they face.

Personal Philosophy

My professional ambitions are driven by a personal philosophy grounded in empathy, reasoning, and continuous learning. I believe that true understanding comes from patient listening and thoughtful analysis. I cultivate these qualities through my personal interests, which include reading, reflective walks, listening to music, and a commitment to lifelong learning.
